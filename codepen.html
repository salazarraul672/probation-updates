Attention Registrants!

1. The purpose of this website is to allow the registrant to submit any required information that needs to be up to date in your file with the local law-enforcement agency where you reside.

2. Submit updates that do not require you to present yourself at our office. Once you submit an update or various updates, they will NOT be automatically added to our system. Once our staff completes updating your file, you will be notified through email and your update status will change from Pending to Completed.



3.To start an update, click on the Submit Updates on the menu above. Once you have entered all the updates, click on Submit
